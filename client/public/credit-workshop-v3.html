Q.01How is this different from the $400/mo providers?

They sell you ongoing access to a portal. We sell you the method itself: vendor sequence, bureau cadence, dashboard, and a finish line. After nine months you own the file. They never let you finish — that's how their pricing works.

Q.02What credit score can I realistically expect?

Most operators reach Paydex 80 by month 4–5 and a strong Intelliscore by month 7 if the cadence is followed. We don't promise outcomes — we promise the method that produces them when worked.

Q.03Do I need to be at a certain revenue?

No. The program works for pre-revenue LLCs that filed last week and for operators with seven figures of monthly throughput. The vendor sequence and bureau cadence are the same.

Q.04What about funding — is that real or a placeholder?

Real. Five processors integrated: Tailored Pay, NMA Gateway, Payarc, PAS Cosmos, Nuvei. Lender matching happens in months 7–9 once your file is fundable.

Q.05Refund policy?
