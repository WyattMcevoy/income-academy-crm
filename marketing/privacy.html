Who we are

Income Academy LLC ("we," "our," "us") operates incomeacademy.biz and related services. If you have questions about this policy, contact us at support@incomeacademy.biz.

What information we collect

Information you provide directly

  • Account & purchase info: name, email address, phone number (optional), and payment details collected through our payment processor (Stripe).
  • Communications: content of any message you send to us, including support tickets and email replies.
  • Profile and content: anything you voluntarily post in our member community.

Information collected automatically

  • Device & usage: IP address, browser type, device type, operating system, pages viewed, date/time of access, referring URL.
  • Cookies and similar technologies: used to keep you logged in, remember preferences, measure site performance, and serve relevant advertising. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some site features may not work.

Information from third parties

  • Payment processor (Stripe): transaction confirmations. We never see or store full credit card numbers.
  • Advertising platforms (Meta, Google): aggregate campaign data.
  • Analytics providers: aggregate visitor behavior.

How we use your information

  • To deliver the products and services you purchased
  • To process payments and send receipts
  • To communicate with you about your account, support requests, and updates
  • To send marketing emails (you can unsubscribe anytime)
  • To measure and improve our site and services
  • To prevent fraud and enforce our Terms of Service
  • To comply with legal obligations

How we share your information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only:

  • With service providers (Stripe, hosting, email delivery, analytics) who help us operate our business, under contractual confidentiality obligations
  • When legally required (court orders, subpoenas, law enforcement requests)
  • To protect rights (fraud prevention, enforcing our Terms)
  • In a business transfer (if we're acquired or merged, your information may be part of the transfer)

Cookies and tracking

We use cookies, pixels, and similar technologies for authentication, preferences, analytics, and advertising. Our advertising partners (including Meta and Google) may place cookies to measure campaign performance and show relevant ads on their platforms. You can opt out of personalized advertising via your device/browser controls and via industry self-regulatory programs.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to:

  • Know what personal information we hold about you
  • Request a copy, correction, or deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of sale or sharing (we don't sell; but you can object to certain sharing)
  • Opt out of marketing emails (via the unsubscribe link in every email)

To exercise any of these rights, email support@incomeacademy.biz. We will respond within 30 days.

California residents (CCPA/CPRA)

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act and Privacy Rights Act, including the right to know, delete, correct, and opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

Children

Our services are not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

Data retention

We retain personal information only as long as needed to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Account information is typically retained while your account is active and for up to 3 years after closure, unless longer retention is required by law.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information. No system is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

International users

Income Academy is operated from the United States. If you access our services from outside the US, your information will be transferred to, stored, and processed in the US.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent change. Significant changes will be communicated via email or a prominent notice on our site.
