Our high-touch program for members who've outgrown solo sourcing. We warehouse your inventory, photograph professionally, list on your behalf, and handle shipping + returns. You focus on finding great inventory — we scale it.

Who this is for

  • You're already consistently sourcing $1,500+ in inventory per month
  • You're spending more time on photography, listing, and shipping than on actual sourcing
  • You want to expand beyond your local sourcing radius
  • You're ready to treat this as a business, not a side hustle

Who this is NOT for

  • Anyone just starting out with reselling (take the Estate Sale Sourcing course in the Foundation Pass first)

  • People not open to a 30-minute phone consultation
