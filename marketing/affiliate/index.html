Module 3: niche selection

Before you commit 12 months — score it

Most affiliate sites fail because people pick the wrong niche. The scorecard evaluates any candidate against 5 criteria. Below is the first one — the full scorecard lives inside Module 3.

1

Audience Fit

Is this an audience I can serve authentically?

1I know nothing about this audience — I'd be faking it
2I could learn but would struggle to build trust
3I have some overlap but not strong personal expertise
4I'm close to this audience (adjacent or past experience)
5I AM this audience — I naturally understand them

Score 3-5 candidate niches across all 5 criteria. Total under 15 = keep looking. You'll save yourself 6-12 wasted months.
