7-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We want you to be confident in your purchase. All Income Academy starter program purchases are covered by our 7-day money-back guarantee.

If within 7 days of your original purchase date you decide Income Academy isn't the right fit, email us and we'll issue a full refund. No arguments, no complicated forms.

How to request a refund

  1. Email support@incomeacademy.biz from the email address you used to purchase
  2. Subject line: Refund Request
  3. Include your order confirmation or receipt
  4. Brief reason (optional but helps us improve)

Refund requests are typically processed within 3 business days. Your refund will be credited back to the original payment method and may take an additional 5-10 business days to appear on your statement, depending on your bank.

After the 7-day period

After 7 days from the original purchase date, all sales are final. We cannot issue refunds for purchases older than 7 days.

What's not refundable

  • Purchases older than 7 days
  • Any separately-purchased add-ons, upsells, or downsells with their own refund terms (see those terms)
  • Accounts terminated for violation of our Terms of Service (e.g., sharing login credentials, recording or distributing our content)

Chargebacks

Please contact us first to resolve any issues. Initiating a chargeback with your bank before contacting us may result in a forfeiture of access and may affect future purchase eligibility. We respond to every refund request — there's no need to escalate to your bank.

Questions

Email support@incomeacademy.biz or use our contact page. We aim to respond within 1 business day.
