Common questions

Can I take more than one course?

Yes — Foundation Pass unlocks all 4. We recommend starting with ONE, finishing it, then evaluating whether to add a second. People who try to do all 4 at once usually finish none.

Which course pays the fastest?

Honestly, Estate Sale Sourcing usually shows revenue first (2–4 weeks) because each item sells on its own. AI Side Income is second (one client lands you paid work). Bookkeeping takes 30–90 days for client onboarding. Affiliate is the slowest but compounds longest. Fast revenue ≠ best fit, though. Pick the one you'll actually work on.

Which course is best for someone over 55?

Bookkeeping From Home is built specifically for the over-50 audience — predictable monthly income, no client-chasing once you're established, work-from-home. That said, plenty of members in the affiliate and AI courses are 55+ and doing well. It's about temperament, not age.

What's the eBay Done-With-You program I see mentioned?

Separate from Foundation Pass. It's a fulfillment service that members of the Estate Sale Sourcing course can apply to after they've been sourcing solo for 6+ months. Application-only. We accept about half who apply. The Foundation Pass itself does not include DWY.

What if I pick wrong and want to switch?

You don't have to "pick" — your $19/mo gives you all four. Start with one, finish it, then move to the next if you want. Most members eventually do 2–3 over the course of a year or two.
