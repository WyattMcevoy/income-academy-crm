# Linda Video Briefs - All 36 Modules

**Purpose:** What each HeyGen Linda video should cover, per module. Each video is the "intro" to its lesson - the written copy carries the step-by-step. Linda's job: hook, teach 1-2 key concepts, motivate.

**Target length:** 3-5 minutes per video. Total HeyGen runtime: ~150 minutes across 36 videos.

**Avatar:** Saffron (HeyGen) presented as Linda - grey hair, glasses, blazer, sitting at table.

**Tone:** Warm. Patient. Specific. Encouraging without being saccharine. Speaks like a 65-year-old mentor would speak to her 60-year-old neighbor.

---

## Production framework (every video)

1. **Hook** (15 sec): Open with the emotional stake or curiosity.
2. **What you'll learn** (30 sec): Preview 3 things this module covers.
3. **Teaching moment** (90-180 sec): 1-2 key concepts demonstrated, not just described.
4. **Why this matters** (30 sec): Stakes for the student.
5. **Call to action** (20 sec): "Read the lesson below, do the homework, see you in Module X."
