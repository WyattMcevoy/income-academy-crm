# AI Asset Generation Prompts

Copy-paste prompts for generating Income Academy visual assets using ChatGPT's image generator (GPT-Image-1), DALL-E 3, Midjourney, Sora, Runway, Veo, Kling, etc. Drop generated files into the paths noted.

**Reminder on identity framing (LVL V1 + FTC)**: These assets are for aspirational lifestyle imagery, not instructor-identity claims. Do not label any AI-generated person as "[YOUR NAME]" or as a customer giving a testimonial. Replace with real photography of you (and real customer testimonials) before public launch.

---

## Target audience baseline

Semi-retired US adults 55-70. Kitchen-table / home-office working style. Warm morning or afternoon light. Realistic, not aspirational-glam. The vibe is "authentic neighbor," not "stock model."

## Brand direction for all imagery

- Warm natural light (morning or golden hour)
- Soft navy accents, cream/white backgrounds, hints of warm gold
- No neon, no futuristic, no robotic, no "startup office"
- Wooden textures, books, coffee, real homes
- No text overlays in the image itself (unless explicitly for OG images)
- Shot like lifestyle editorial, not stock

## 1. AI Side Income — hero image

**Destination**: `marketing/brand/hero/ai-hero.jpg` AND `marketing/ai/img/hero.jpg`
**Spec**: 1600×900 JPG, under 400KB

**Prompt**:
```
Photorealistic lifestyle portrait of a 62-year-old woman with a warm genuine smile, sitting at a clean light-wood kitchen table with a MacBook or laptop open in front of her. Morning sunlight pours in from a window at her left, casting soft warm shadows. A white ceramic coffee mug rests beside the laptop. She is curious and engaged, looking slightly down at the screen, not posed. Cream-colored shirt, no visible branding. Kitchen is uncluttered but lived-in — a bowl of fruit in soft focus background. She has natural grey-silver hair, slight laugh lines, honest eyes. Shot at 35mm, shallow depth of field. Warm color palette: soft whites, natural wood tones, a hint of warm navy from her shirt trim. Composition: horizontal 16:9, subject slightly left of center, negative space to the right. No text overlays. No watermarks.
```

**Alt variations to try**:
- Male subject (65-year-old man, same setting)
- Couple (both at the table, one writing, one on the laptop)
- Different room (small home study with bookshelf)

## 2. AI Side Income — hero video

**Destination**: `marketing/brand/hero/ai-hero.mp4` AND `marketing/ai/video/hero.mp4`
**Spec**: 1920×1080 H.264 MP4, 30-90 seconds, 30 fps, no audio (add voice-over/music in post)

**Prompt for Sora / Veo / Runway**:
```
Cinematic b-roll sequence, 60 seconds, warm morning light throughout:

Shot 1 (0-8s): Wide establishing shot of a sunny home kitchen. Light streams through sheer curtains. A laptop is open on a wooden table.

Shot 2 (8-18s): Medium shot of a 62-year-old woman (grey-silver hair, warm smile) sitting down at the table, opening her laptop, taking a sip of coffee from a white mug.

Shot 3 (18-30s): Close-up of her hands typing on the laptop keyboard, unhurried, confident.

Shot 4 (30-45s): Over-the-shoulder angle as she reads something on screen and smiles slightly — not exaggerated, a real reaction.

Shot 5 (45-60s): Pull back to wide shot of the kitchen as morning light shifts. She sips coffee, relaxed.

Pacing: slow, patient, unhurried. Shot on a cinema camera, 35mm lens, shallow DoF. Color grade: warm, natural, slight film grain. No on-screen text. No dialogue. No music in the export. Kitchen is uncluttered but lived-in.
```

## Output quality checklist

Before saving a generated asset:
- [ ] Check for AI artifacts: hands (extra fingers), eyes (asymmetry), text/logos (garbled)
- [ ] Color palette matches navy/gold/cream — no out-of-brand colors (teal, purple, bright red)
- [ ] Subject looks authentically like the demographic (55-70, not 30-something actor)
- [ ] Lighting feels natural and warm, not over-saturated
- [ ] No visible watermarks from the generator
- [ ] File size within spec (optimize with squoosh.app or similar if needed)
- [ ] Filename matches exactly what `index.html` references

## When you're ready for real photography

These prompts double as a shoot brief. When you commission or record real content, follow the same direction (subject age, lighting, setting, wardrobe, pacing). The HTML references stable filenames so swapping AI-generated for real-person is a drop-in replacement — no code changes.
