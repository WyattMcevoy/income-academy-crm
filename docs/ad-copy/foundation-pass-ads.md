# Foundation Pass — Ad Copy Library

20 ad variants for Facebook / Instagram / Google Ads, backed by the competitive research findings. Each variant is CTA-ready.

**Compliance rules**:
- No earnings guarantees
- No "passive income in 30 days" language (FTC Operation AI Comply bans this)
- No manufactured urgency
- Audience-appropriate: sober, direct, not hype-y
- Disclaimer link implied in landing page, not needed in ad itself

**Landing page per ad set**:
- Generic Foundation Pass ads → `https://incomeacademy.biz`
- AI-specific ads → `https://ai.incomeacademy.biz`
- Affiliate-specific ads → `https://affiliate.incomeacademy.biz`

**Targeting suggestion** (Facebook/Instagram):
- Age: 55-75
- Geography: US (English-speaking, highest converting states: FL, AZ, TX, CA, PA, NC, OH)
- Interests: depending on variant — retirement planning, online business, side hustle (avoid "MLM", "crypto", "affiliate marketing" which attract scam-seeker crowd)
- Exclude: anyone who's visited the refund page, anyone in the Foundation Pass buyers list

## Ad creative direction

### Images / video

- Use brand colors (navy + gold). Never stock-photo-hand-shaking energy.
- Subject of imagery: age-appropriate (55-75), at-home-settings (kitchen, home office), natural candid lighting
- NO photos of money, lottery tickets, Lamborghinis, or "lifestyle" imagery
- NO before/after income screenshots (FTC risk, plus it reads as scam to this audience)
- Ideal: simple text-overlay cards using the brand typography — feels serious, not salesy

### Colors
- Background: navy (`#0f172a`) or cream (`#f8fafc`)
- Accent: gold (`#f59e0b`)
- Never: bright red, yellow-on-yellow, high-saturation colors

### Logo placement
- Small IA mark in corner
- Never as the hero element

## Testing recommendation

Run a 5-variant pack across ~$200-500 ad spend per audience before scaling. Track:
- CTR (click-through rate) — 1-2% is solid for this audience
- CPC (cost per click) — aim under $3 in US
- Conversion rate on landing page — 2-4% is good, 5%+ is great
- Cost per checkout — target under $80 for a $47 + $19/mo product

Kill variants performing below 50% of the pack median after 1,000 impressions. Scale winners.
