# Estate Sale & Garage Sale Sourcing Academy

**Status**: scaffolding — full prompts/templates/scripts pending
**Slot**: Course 3 of 4 in the Income Academy Foundation Pass bundle
**Format**: 8-module video course + downloadable checklists + sourcing worksheets
**Positioning**: the direct feeder into the eBay Reselling Done-With-You graduation tier

## Why this course exists

Estate Sale Sourcing is the single strongest feeder into the eBay Reselling $2-5k high-ticket program. Every other bundled course produces standalone income — this one produces **inventory that the eBay program consumes**. The value ladder story writes itself: "You're finding great deals. Now let us warehouse, ship, and scale them for you."

## What buyers get

- 8-module core training on finding, evaluating, buying, and flipping estate sale + garage sale finds
- Category playbooks for the 7 highest-ROI product types (vintage tools, jewelry, collectibles, electronics, glassware, books, furniture)
- Sourcing route-planner spreadsheet for optimizing Saturday mornings
- Pricing + negotiation scripts
- Pipeline to flip (eBay, Facebook Marketplace, Mercari, Whatnot)
- Clear "When to apply to our Done-With-You program" framing in Module 6

## Audience fit (why this wins for 55-75)

- Monetizes behavior retirees already do (Saturday morning estate sales)
- Zero tech skills required for sourcing
- Tangible skill — "you're buying a rug, not crypto"
- Low production cost on our side (field shoots at 3-4 real estate sales + talking-head bridges)
- Lowest FTC risk in the bundle — teaches a shopping/negotiation skill, not an income claim
