## When to expand your operation

Three signs you're ready:

### Sign 1: You're consistently profitable

For 3+ months in a row, you've cleared $1,000+ profit per month. Your numbers are real, not lucky.

### Sign 2: You have time you're not using

You're sourcing 1 day per week and could do 2. Or you're listing 3 hours per week and could do 6. **Capacity, not desperation.**

### Sign 3: Your storage and workflow can handle more

You're not overwhelmed by current inventory. You ship within 2 days. You don't dread the workload.

If all 3 are true -> consider scaling.

If even 1 is false -> stay where you are. Master your current level first.

---

## Three ways to scale

### Way 1: More sourcing days

Add a second day per week. Maybe Friday morning at sales (less competition). Or Sunday for half-off picks.

### Way 2: Wider radius

If you've been doing 30-minute drives, expand to 60 or 90 minutes for high-quality sales.

This adds drive time but often produces 2x to 3x better inventory.

### Way 3: More listings per week

Instead of listing 5 items per week, list 15. This requires:

- Better workflow (batch photography, batch listing)
- Possibly hiring help (next module)
- Better storage organization

---

## When NOT to scale

Stop and think before scaling if:

- **You're stressed.** More volume won't fix stress - it'll multiply it.
- **You're not actually profitable.** Scaling losses just multiplies losses.
- **You're not enjoying it anymore.** Forced fun is bad fun.
- **Your storage is already maxed.** Solve that first.
- **Your shipping workflow is broken.** Slow shipping kills eBay rankings.

**The students who succeed long-term are the ones who scale slowly and carefully.**

## The eBay Done-With-You graduation program

This is our high-touch tier for students who've outgrown solo sourcing.

### What it is

You source. We do everything else.

Specifically:
- We provide warehouse space (you ship inventory to us)
- We photograph items in our studio
- We write listings
- We list, manage, and ship
- We handle returns and customer service

You receive a payout based on net profit (after our service fee).

### Who it's for

People who:

- Are consistently sourcing $1,500+ profit per month solo
- Want to spend 100% of their time sourcing (no listing/shipping)
- Want to expand inventory volume by 5x to 10x
- Are comfortable with our service taking a percentage cut

### Who it's NOT for

People who:

- Haven't proven profitability solo yet
- Want to stay small (under $2,000/month)
- Don't have the time to source 2 to 3 days per week
- Want to keep 100% of profits

### What it costs

- Application is free
- We charge a percentage of NET sales (after eBay fees, before our cut)
- Specific structure is reviewed in the application call

**Most successful students net $4,000 to $12,000+ per month** in this program. The trade-off is our cut.

### How to apply

1. You need to have 6+ months of consistent solo sourcing data
2. Apply via the link in your Foundation Pass dashboard
3. We do a 30-minute application call to assess fit
4. If accepted, onboarding takes about 2 weeks

We accept students by application only. **We turn down about half who apply** - because we want to make sure it's actually a fit.

## The 90-day decision framework

By the time you've done estate sale sourcing for 90 days, you should know:

### Can I see myself doing this for 2+ years?

If yes -> consider scaling.
If no -> wrap up your current inventory and move to a different course.

### Am I making at least $1,500 profit per month consistently?

If yes -> you're ready to consider scaling.
If no -> there's still optimization to do at your current scale.

### Do I want to do MORE work, or just more efficient work?

More work + same effort -> apply for DWY (we do the listing).
More efficient work + same income -> stay solo, refine your process.

### Do I want to keep doing the listing/shipping work myself?

If yes -> stay solo, don't apply for DWY.
If no -> DWY is your path.

## If you got stuck

### "I'm 90 days in but only making $500/month profit"

Don't scale yet. Two possible issues:

1. **Niche/category too narrow.** Module 3 review.
2. **Pricing too low or sales too slow.** Module 4 review.
3. **Inventory not actually selling.** Module 5 review.

Spend the next 30 days fixing the core, not adding volume.

### "I want to apply for DWY but I'm not sure I qualify"

Apply anyway. The application call helps clarify. **The worst outcome is 'come back in 3 months.'**

### "I want a real person to review my numbers and advise"

Email **support@incomeacademy.biz** with your last 3 months of profit/loss. **Free 30-minute coaching call included with your Foundation Pass.**
