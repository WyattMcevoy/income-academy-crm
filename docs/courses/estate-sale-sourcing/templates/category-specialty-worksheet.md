# Category Specialty Worksheet — Estate Sale Sourcing, Module 3

**Goal**: by the end of this worksheet you have picked 1–2 categories you'll specialize in for your first 90 days, with a justified reason for each.

**Time required**: 30–40 minutes.

**Why specialize:** generalist resellers spend hours pricing items they don't understand. Specialists know at a glance what's worth $5 vs $500. Pick narrowly first, expand later.

---

## Part 5 — What "done" looks like

You can go to an estate sale this weekend with:
- A clear shopping list (your 2 categories)
- A clear NOT-buying list (categories you'll skip)
- A price ceiling you trust
- Recognition for 3+ brands/makers in each chosen category

If you'd freeze up at a sale because "everything looks interesting," you haven't narrowed enough. Go back to Part 1 and force-rank stricter.
