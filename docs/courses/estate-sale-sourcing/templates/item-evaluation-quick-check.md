# 30-Second Item Evaluation Checklist

Use this at the sale when you're not sure whether to buy. Print it, laminate it, keep it in your phone notes.

---

## Step 1 — The 50/50 Rule (10 seconds)

Can I reasonably expect to sell this for AT LEAST 2x the asking price, after eBay fees (~13%) and shipping costs?

| Asking price | Required sell price |
|---|---|
| $5 | $12+ |
| $10 | $25+ |
| $20 | $50+ |
| $30 | $75+ |
| $50 | $125+ |
| $100 | $250+ |

**Yes** → continue to Step 2
**No** → walk away (unless emotional attachment warrants it)

---

## Step 2 — The 30-Second Phone Check

Open eBay on your phone. Search: `[ITEM NAME] sold`. Filter: Sold Listings + past 90 days.

- How many recent sales? ______ (under 5 = thin market, skip)
- Price range on comparable condition: $______ to $______
- Median sell price: ~$______

Does my asking price × 2 fit within this sold range? **Yes / No**

---

## Step 3 — Physical Inspection (60 seconds)

Walk around the item and check:

- [ ] **Structural**: cracks, chips, breaks? Count each.
- [ ] **Completeness**: all pieces, lids, attachments, original parts?
- [ ] **Maker's mark**: can I verify authenticity (stamps, labels, etc.)?
- [ ] **Era indicators**: does the construction/material match claimed era?
- [ ] **Sympathetic vs. bad repair**: any attempts to fix? Do they reduce value?
- [ ] **Smell test**: smoke / mildew / pet odor that won't come out?

**Clean** → proceed to Step 4
**Any fatal flaw** → skip

---

## Step 4 — The Emotional Trap Check (5 seconds)

Answer honestly:
- Am I buying this because it'll make money, or because I LIKE it personally?
- Have I already driven 45 minutes to this sale and feel like I need to buy something?

If the honest answer is yes to either, **be more conservative** on the decision. These are real cognitive biases that cost resellers money.

---

## Decision

- **Yes, buy at asking**: confident sell at 2x+ within 60 days, no flaws, real category expertise
- **Yes, negotiate down**: confident on sell price, asking is tight on 50/50 rule, try 15-25% off
- **Yes, last-hour only**: borderline buy, come back at half-price day if still here
- **No, walk away**: insufficient margin, fatal flaw, or unclear category

---

## Optional — Photograph it before leaving

Even if you walk away, take a photo. If it's still there 2 hours later or at half price tomorrow, you already know what it is. Building a mental database of "items I passed on" is free category-expertise training.
