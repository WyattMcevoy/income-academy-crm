# Privacy Policy — Starter Template

**⚠️ Not legal advice.** This is a starter template. Every site should have an attorney review for specific jurisdiction, services, and data practices before publishing.

---

```
# Privacy Policy

**Last updated**: [DATE]

## Who we are

This Privacy Policy describes how [YOUR SITE/BUSINESS NAME] ("we," "our," "us") collects, uses, and handles your personal information when you visit [YOUR_SITE_URL] or interact with our services.

Contact us with privacy questions: [support@yourdomain.com]
Mailing address: [YOUR MAILING ADDRESS — virtual mailbox is fine]

## What we collect

### Information you provide
- **Email address** when you subscribe to our newsletter or download a lead magnet
- **Name** if provided with your email
- **Comments** you post on our site
- **Payment information** only when purchasing (processed by our payment processor, not stored by us)
- **Support messages** you send us

### Information collected automatically
- **Device and usage data**: IP address, browser type, operating system, pages viewed, time spent, referring URL
- **Cookies** (see Cookie section below)
- **Analytics data** via Google Analytics (anonymized)

### Information from third parties
- **Affiliate networks** may provide aggregated reporting about referred purchases (not individual data)
- **Email marketing tools** may share engagement data (opens, clicks)

## How we use your information

- Deliver services you signed up for (emails, downloads, products)
- Respond to support requests
- Send marketing emails (you can unsubscribe anytime)
- Improve our website and content
- Prevent fraud and enforce our Terms
- Comply with legal obligations

## How we share your information

We do not sell your personal information. We share only:

- **With service providers** (email platform, hosting, analytics, payment processor) under contract to handle data appropriately
- **Affiliate networks** — no personal data shared; only click and conversion tracking (anonymized)
- **When legally required** (court orders, law enforcement)
- **To protect rights** (preventing fraud, enforcing Terms)
- **In a business transfer** (if we're acquired, your data may transfer)

## Cookies and tracking

We use cookies for:
- Keeping you signed in (functional)
- Remembering preferences (functional)
- Measuring site performance (analytics)
- Tracking affiliate clicks (commercial)

You can disable cookies in your browser. Some site features may not work.

We use the following tracking tools:
- **Google Analytics** (anonymized)
- **[List any others: Meta Pixel, email tracking, etc.]**

## Affiliate relationships

This site contains affiliate links. When you click them and purchase, we may earn a commission at no cost to you. These clicks are tracked by the affiliate program; purchases are reported to us in aggregate (we don't see individual credit card info).

See our [FTC disclosure] for specific product relationships.

## Your rights

### General rights
- **Access**: request a copy of personal data we hold about you
- **Correction**: update incorrect information
- **Deletion**: request removal (subject to legal retention requirements)
- **Opt-out of marketing**: unsubscribe from emails anytime
- **Withdraw consent**: revoke prior consent

### California residents (CCPA/CPRA)
California residents have additional rights:
- Right to know what personal information we hold
- Right to delete personal information
- Right to correct inaccurate information
- Right to opt out of "sale" or "sharing" (we do not sell — we share only as described above)
- Right not to be discriminated against for exercising rights

To exercise, email [support@yourdomain.com] with subject "CA Privacy Request."

### EU/EEA/UK residents (GDPR)
If you're in the EU, EEA, or UK:
- Right of access, rectification, erasure (right to be forgotten)
- Right to restrict processing
- Right to data portability
- Right to object to processing
- Right to not be subject to automated decision-making

Legal basis for processing:
- **Consent** for marketing emails
- **Legitimate interest** for analytics and service improvement
- **Contract performance** for delivering purchased products
- **Legal obligation** for compliance

Request data or exercise rights by emailing [support@yourdomain.com].

If you're not satisfied with our response, you may file a complaint with your data protection authority.

## Children's privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us immediately.

## Data retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary:
- Newsletter subscribers: until you unsubscribe (plus 30 days for suppression list)
- Customers: as long as account is active, plus up to 3 years for business records
- Support communications: up to 2 years
- Compliance-required records (tax, legal): as required by law

## Data security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:
- HTTPS encryption for data in transit
- Access controls for authorized personnel
- Regular security reviews

No method is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## International data transfers

Our servers are located in [USA / other country]. If you access our services from outside [COUNTRY], your data will be transferred to and processed in [COUNTRY].

For EU residents: we rely on [Standard Contractual Clauses / Data Privacy Framework / appropriate safeguards] for such transfers.

## Third-party links

This site contains links to third-party websites (affiliate partners, resources). We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Review their policies separately.

## Do Not Track

Our site does not currently respond to Do Not Track browser signals.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects recent changes. For material changes, we'll notify subscribers via email.

## Contact us

Privacy questions or requests:
- Email: [support@yourdomain.com]
- Mail: [YOUR MAILING ADDRESS]
- Subject line for rights requests: "[CA/EU] Privacy Request" or "Data Access Request"

---

*This privacy policy was drafted using standard templates and should be customized to your specific business and reviewed by an attorney familiar with privacy law in your jurisdiction.*
```

## Where to display

1. **Linked in footer of every page** (required by most jurisdictions)
2. **Linked from every form** that collects data (email opt-ins, contact forms)
3. **Linked from purchase pages** before checkout

## Companion documents often required alongside

- **Terms of Service** — what visitors agree to by using the site
- **Cookie Policy** — detailed cookie usage (can be merged into Privacy Policy)
- **Affiliate Disclosure** — FTC-compliant affiliate relationships (see `ftc-disclosure-library.md`)
- **Earnings Disclaimer** — required when making any income-related claims

## Update cadence

Review privacy policy at least annually. Major updates:
- Change in data practices (new tool, new data collected)
- New regulations (new state privacy laws keep coming)
- Change in business structure
