# Module 0: Welcome and Reality Check

**Time:** about 20 minutes
**What you'll do:** Get clear on what affiliate marketing really is, what it isn't, and whether it's a good fit for you.

---

## Welcome

I'm so glad you're here.

Before we go any further, let's make sure you and I are on the same page about what affiliate marketing actually is - because most courses lie to you about this, and I refuse to.

Here's the honest, plain-English version:

> **Affiliate marketing means you build something useful (a website, a YouTube channel, or a newsletter), people come to trust your judgment, you recommend products that genuinely help them, and you get paid a small commission when they buy.**

That's it.

There's no magic button. No loophole. No "passive income on autopilot."

It's a real business that takes 6 to 24 months to start producing meaningful income - but once it does, it can produce income for years. **That's the deal.**

If you're still in after reading that, you're going to do well in this course.

---

## Why this works for our age group

People over 50 have a huge advantage in affiliate marketing.

Why? **Trust.**

The whole game in affiliate marketing is convincing strangers on the internet that your recommendation is real and not just a sales pitch. That's hard for a 25-year-old to do convincingly.

It's much easier for someone with 30 years of life experience writing in their actual voice about products they actually use. **Your wisdom is the asset.** AI tools just give you the speed to publish more often.

Real affiliate marketers in their 50s and 60s are out there making $5,000 to $15,000 per month from a single content site. It takes patience. But the math works.

## A word on what's realistic

Setting you up for success, not surprises. Here's what you should actually expect:

- **Months 1 to 3:** building. Setting up your site/channel/newsletter. Picking your niche. Writing your first 10 to 20 pieces of content. **Income: $0 (this is normal).**

- **Months 4 to 9:** finding your voice. Traffic starts trickling in. First affiliate clicks. **Income: $0 to $200 per month (this is normal).**

- **Months 10 to 18:** the curve starts bending. Search engines start trusting you. Your audience starts growing. **Income: $200 to $1,500 per month.**

- **Months 19 to 24:** real income. **$1,500 to $5,000 per month is realistic** if you've been consistent.

- **Year 3+:** for the students who don't quit, this becomes a meaningful income stream that requires only 5 to 10 hours per week to maintain.

The hardest part is the first 12 months when you're working without income. **The students who succeed are the ones who treat months 1 through 12 as paid education.** They'd be paying $30,000 for an MBA - this is cheaper.

## Your "why" statement

Before you start the rest of the course, do this one thing.

Open a notebook (or a Word doc, or a sticky note). Answer this question in 1 to 3 sentences:

> **Why am I really doing this?**

Some examples:

- "I want to retire from my office job 3 years earlier than my pension allows. $2,000/month from this would do it."
- "I want a business that doesn't require me to leave my house, because I take care of my husband full-time."
- "I love woodworking and I'm tired of giving free advice. I want to monetize the audience I've built on Facebook."
- "I just want a creative project that pays its own way and gives me something to do."

Write yours. **Tape it above your desk.**

When months 4 through 8 feel slow and you start asking "is this even worth it?" - **read your why statement.** That's the whole point.

## If you got stuck

### "I don't know what my why is yet"

That's okay. Try this prompt instead: "If this worked, what would change in my life 2 years from now?"

Even one specific answer (for example, "I'd quit my customer service job") is enough to start.

### "I'm worried I'm too late to start affiliate marketing"

You're not. The internet has gotten BIGGER, not smaller. People over 50 are one of the fastest-growing online demographics. **Your audience is just getting started.**

The advantage you have over a 25-year-old: you've actually used products for decades and can talk about them with real authority.

### "Can I do this AND the AI Side Income course at the same time?"

You can - but we don't recommend it for the first 90 days. **Pick one and focus.** Most students who try to do both end up doing neither.

If you've already done well in AI Side Income (landed clients, building income), you can layer this in starting around month 3 or 4.
