## FAQ

**Do I need to be good with computers?**
You need to be comfortable with email, typing, and using a web browser. If you can navigate Facebook, Gmail, or online banking, you can use AI tools. We cover the "how do I set this up" steps in Module 1.

**Is this the same AI everyone's scared of?**
Yes and no. "AI" in the news is often overhyped or under-explained. The tools we use (ChatGPT, Claude) are just really good at writing, summarizing, and organizing. They won't replace you. They'll make the work you do faster and better. Module 1 explains exactly what they are.

**How long will it take to finish the course?**
Most people finish the core material in 1-2 weeks at 30-45 min/day. The material stays in your account — revisit anytime.

**How quickly can I start making money?**
That depends entirely on you. The course teaches a 90-day plan. Some members land their first client in week 3. Some take 3 months. Many take longer. We don't promise outcomes. What we promise is a real framework.

## Notes for the copywriter

1. **Lead with honesty.** This demographic has been burned by too many "push-button profits" offers. Sobriety is the differentiator.
2. **Use specific numbers.** "$300-500 per article" beats "great income potential."
3. **Name the obvious objection before they raise it.** "I don't know if I'm too old for this" — we address it directly.
4. **Show the instructor.** This demo trusts people, not brands.
5. **Keep the CTA consistent.** Same button text everywhere. No confusion about what "click here" does.
