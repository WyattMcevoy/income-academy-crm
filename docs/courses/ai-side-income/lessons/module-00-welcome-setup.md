### Tool 1: ChatGPT

ChatGPT is the most popular AI chat tool. It's made by a company called OpenAI. We'll use it for many of the side hustles in Module 3, especially writing tasks.

#### Step-by-step signup

1. **Open your web browser.**
   - That's the program you use to get on the internet — Safari, Chrome, Firefox, or Edge.
   - If you're not sure, look at the bottom of your screen for an icon. Safari is a blue compass. Chrome is a colored circle (red/yellow/green/blue). Click on it.

2. **Find the address bar.** That's the long white box at the top of the browser window. It's where you'd normally type a website name.

3. **Click once inside the address bar.** Any text in there should highlight. If it doesn't, press **Cmd+A** (Mac) or **Ctrl+A** (Windows) to select everything, then press **Delete**.

4. **Type the following exactly, then press the Enter key** (the big key on the right side of your keyboard):

   ```
   chat.openai.com
   ```

5. **You'll see a page titled "Welcome to ChatGPT"** with two big buttons: **"Log in"** and **"Sign up"**.
   - Click **"Sign up"** (the blue one).

6. **Choose how to sign up.** You'll see options like:
   - Continue with Google
   - Continue with Microsoft
   - Continue with Apple
   - Or enter your email address

   **The fastest way**: if you have a Gmail address (like `yourname@gmail.com`), click **"Continue with Google"**. It'll show you a list of Gmail accounts you're already logged into in your browser. Click the one you want to use.

   **If you don't have Gmail**, click "Continue with email." It'll ask for an email address and a password. Use an email you actually check, because they'll send you a verification message.

7. **Verify your email** (only if you signed up with email, not Google):
   - Open a new tab in your browser (Cmd+T or Ctrl+T)
   - Go to your email
   - Find a message from OpenAI — subject line will say something like "Verify your email"
   - Click the big button inside that says "Verify email address"
   - You'll be redirected back to ChatGPT, signed in.

8. **Answer the welcome questions.** It'll ask for your name and birthday. Answer honestly. They'll also ask if you want to give them your phone number — you can skip this if you'd rather not.

9. **You're in.** You should now see a clean page with a chat box at the bottom that says **"Message ChatGPT…"**

#### Your first conversation with ChatGPT

1. **Click inside that chat box** at the bottom of the page where it says "Message ChatGPT…"

2. **Type a question.** Anything you'd ask a friend who happens to know everything. Some good starters:

   - `Explain inflation to me like I'm a smart 12-year-old.`
   - `What's the difference between a Roth IRA and a Traditional IRA?`
   - `I have chicken, rice, broccoli, and a half a bottle of soy sauce. What can I make for dinner?`
   - `Write me a 3-paragraph thank-you note to my doctor.`

3. **Press Enter on your keyboard** (or click the up-arrow button on the right of the chat box).

4. **Watch the response appear, word by word.** This is normal — that's just how ChatGPT works. It's not broken. It's "thinking."

5. **Reply.** Ask a follow-up. "Can you give me an example?" or "Make it shorter." or "What about for someone in their 60s?"

6. **Keep going for at least 10 minutes.** The point isn't to get useful answers — it's to feel comfortable with the interface. You're going to live here for the next 8 weeks.

### Tool 2: Claude

Claude is the second AI chat tool we'll use. It's made by a company called Anthropic. Many users find Claude is better at writing tasks specifically — its tone is usually a bit more thoughtful. We'll use it heavily in Modules 5 through 8.

#### Step-by-step signup

1. **Open a new tab in your browser.** Press **Cmd+T** (Mac) or **Ctrl+T** (Windows). A blank new tab will open.

2. **In the address bar, type:**

   ```
   claude.ai
   ```

3. **Press Enter.**

4. **You'll see a page titled "Claude"** with a "Continue with Google" or "Sign up with email" option, just like ChatGPT.

5. **Sign up the same way you did with ChatGPT.** If you used Google for ChatGPT, use Google for Claude too — it'll be one click. If you used email, use the same email you used for ChatGPT (so you don't have two passwords to keep track of).

6. **Verify your email** if you signed up with email (same process as ChatGPT — check your inbox for a verification message).

7. **You're in.** You should now see a chat box that says **"How can I help you today?"** at the top, with a text input at the bottom.

#### Your first conversation with Claude

1. **Type a question.** Try the **same** question you asked ChatGPT — for example: `Explain inflation to me like I'm a smart 12-year-old.`

2. **Press Enter.**

3. **Read the answer.** You'll notice Claude's response is slightly different — sometimes a bit more careful, sometimes a bit more conversational.

4. **Have at least a 10-minute conversation.** Same idea — get comfortable with the interface.

### Now: notice what's different (5 minutes)

Here's a quick experiment to wrap up:

1. **Open both ChatGPT and Claude side by side in two browser tabs.**

2. **Pick a question you actually care about.** Examples:
   - `What's a good first online business for someone over 60 who's never done one before?`
   - `If I have 10 hours a week and want to make an extra $1,500 a month, what should I focus on?`
   - `Write me a cold outreach email to a local realtor offering to write their LinkedIn posts.`

3. **Paste the same question into both** ChatGPT and Claude.

4. **Read both answers carefully.** Notice:
   - Where they agree
   - Where they give different advice
   - Which one's tone feels more like a person you'd want to work with
   - Which one's answer is more useful to *you specifically*

You now know more about AI tools than 90% of people your age. **And you've only been here 25 minutes.**

## If you got stuck

It happens to everyone. Here are the three most common things that go wrong:

### "I forgot my password"
Click "Log in" → click "Forgot password?" → enter your email → check your inbox for a reset link.

### "I'm getting an error message I don't understand"
Take a screenshot (Cmd+Shift+4 on Mac, Win+Shift+S on Windows) and send it to support@incomeacademy.biz with one sentence about what you were trying to do. We'll respond within 24 hours.

### "ChatGPT or Claude is giving me weird, broken answers"
Refresh the page (Cmd+R or Ctrl+R) and try again. AI tools occasionally have hiccups.

### "It's asking me to pay"
Both tools have a free tier. If you see a "Pro" or "Plus" upgrade prompt — **don't pay yet.** The free tier is enough for everything in this course. We'll talk about when (and if) to upgrade in Module 5.

### "I'm stuck for more than 15 minutes"
Email support@incomeacademy.biz with a screenshot of what you're seeing. We'll walk you through it. **Do not skip this lesson** — Module 1 assumes both accounts are set up.
