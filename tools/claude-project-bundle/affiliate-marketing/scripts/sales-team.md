# Sales Team Script — Honest Affiliate Marketing Starter → Content Site Accelerator

For phone/Zoom sales reps closing $47 buyers into the $2-5k "Content Site Accelerator" high-ticket back-end.

**Goal**: 12-18% close rate on qualified conversations. $2,997 average order value.

Slightly lower close rate than AI course because affiliate marketing buyers are more risk-aware, longer-horizon thinkers. But they're also stickier — they don't disappear after 30 days.

---

## Before the call (5 min)

- Pull buyer's CRM record (how far in the course, purchase date, what they paid)
- Check GHL: how much of the starter content have they consumed?
- Check MailerLite: email engagement pattern
- Note: what did they mark as their niche choice during intake? Their path (content/video/email)?
- Have the Accelerator offer sheet ready
- Have the Stripe Payment Link ready

---

## Call structure

### 1. Opening (3 min)

```
"Hi [FIRST NAME] — this is [YOUR NAME] from Income Academy. Following up since you picked up the Affiliate Starter [N DAYS] ago. Got a few minutes to talk about how it's going?"

[If yes, continue. If no, book.]

"Ground rule — this isn't going to be a 30-minute pitch. I'm going to ask you questions about what you're working on. Then if we might be a fit to work more closely, I'll tell you. If not, I'll tell you that too. Sound good?"
```

### 2. Discovery (10-15 min)

```
"First — why affiliate marketing? What pulled you toward this specifically?"

[LISTEN. Pay attention to: past attempts, timeline expectations, what success looks like to them.]

"What have you tried before? Any prior attempts at online business?"

"How far are you in the course material?"
[If Module 3+, more qualified. If still in Module 1, slower approach — let them finish first.]

"Have you picked a niche yet? If so, walk me through why that one."
[Their niche choice tells you everything. If they picked a saturated niche, they haven't internalized Module 3.]

"What's your realistic timeline for wanting to see results? When do you hope to be earning?"
[Listen for: 3 months = unrealistic; 12-18 months = sober; 2+ years = very patient, probably a good fit]

"What's going to get in the way? Be honest. Time? Technical stuff? Writing consistency? Picking products?"

[They're diagnosing their own problem. Write it down.]
```

### 3. Teaching moment (5 min)

Give them ONE insight based on what they told you. Examples:

**If they picked a saturated niche:**
> "Quick thing on your niche choice. Let me look — [NICHE] — the issue with this one is: look at the top 10 Google results for your main keyword. It's probably all massive sites — Healthline, Wirecutter, Forbes. A small site can't realistically break into that top 10 within 12 months. 
> 
> What I'd suggest: find a sub-niche within that space. Not 'fitness' — 'strength training for men over 60.' Not 'cooking' — 'air fryer cooking for empty nesters.' You need to go narrow enough that the competition is small sites like yours, not billion-dollar publishers.
> 
> Have you thought about a sub-niche? What's one you'd genuinely care about 2 years from now?"

**If they said "I don't have time to write that much":**
> "Real talk — affiliate marketing takes about 10-15 hours of writing per week to build momentum in the first 6 months. It's a content business. If 10-15 hours isn't in your calendar, you have three options:
> 
> 1. Cut scope — do 3-5 hours per week and accept it'll take 2x longer
> 2. Hire a writer (typical: $50-100 per article, you edit for voice)
> 3. Pick a different model entirely (our AI Side Income course is more service-based)
> 
> Which one fits your situation?"

**If they said "I've tried before and it didn't work":**
> "That's actually common among our successful members — they all tried something before. What specifically broke down? Was it niche selection, content quality, traffic, or just quitting too early?
> 
> [LISTEN — they'll self-diagnose]
> 
> Here's what makes the difference: most people who 'try' affiliate marketing quit at month 3 because they haven't seen results yet. The people who win are the ones who keep going past month 6. That's the whole game."

### 4. Transition to the offer

Only if fit is clear.

```
"Based on what you've told me, you might be a good fit for our deeper program — the Content Site Accelerator. Let me tell you about it briefly, then you decide.

It's a 90-day done-with-you program where we help you actually build your first affiliate site from scratch to first commissions:

Week 1-2: Niche validation with a coach, keyword research, site setup
Week 3-6: Write and publish your first 10 articles with weekly coaching + edits
Week 7-10: Traffic building — SEO, guest posting, email list start
Week 11-12: Review, optimize, plan next 90 days

You also get:
- Weekly 1:1 calls with a coach who's built and sold affiliate sites
- Edit pass on your first 5 articles (we literally fix them before you publish)
- Custom niche validation — we tell you honestly if your niche choice is strong or not
- Our private affiliate site builder community
- All future Starter Course updates forever
- Our 'revive dead articles' playbook (worth $500 alone for anyone with older content)

Investment: $2,997. Or payment plan: $1,200 down, $700/month for 3 months.

People do this when:
- They've been through the Starter Kit and want the accountability to actually execute
- They've tried and stopped and want to not repeat the mistake
- They have 10-20 hours/week available for 90 days

Does that fit where you are?"
```

### 5. Objection handling

**"That's a lot of money for something that takes 6-12 months to earn back."**

```
"Fair. Let me put it this way: you're already convinced affiliate marketing can work — you bought the Starter. The question isn't 'does it work' — it's 'am I going to actually execute?' 

Most people who buy a $47 course don't execute. They read half the modules and stop. Not because the course is wrong — because they don't have structure.

The Accelerator is the structure. Weekly calls, edits on your work, niche validation. It's what turns 'I bought a course' into 'I built a site that earns.'

The $3k, spread over 3 months with the payment plan, is less than what most people spend on unused gym memberships, unused software subscriptions, and unfinished Udemy courses in a year.

If you execute the Accelerator, your site will probably earn $200-1,000/month by month 12. If you execute alone, it might. The difference is you don't risk being in the 80% who don't execute."
```

**"I want to try the Starter Kit alone first."**

```
"Totally fair. How far are you right now? [LISTEN]

Here's my suggestion: if you're through Module 4, you've got the framework. The Accelerator accelerates the DOING. If you're earlier than Module 4, yes, finish those first.

Let's do this — send me a note when you hit Module 6 (traffic strategy). I'll hold a spot open for 30 days, no pressure. If you're crushing it alone by then, ignore me. If you've hit a wall, we'll talk."
```

**"I need to think about it."**

```
"Of course. What specifically — the money, the time commitment, or something else?

[LET THEM ANSWER]

If money: let's talk about the payment plan.
If time: let's be honest about whether NOW is the right moment. If you're in a crunch at work or at home, maybe it's 2 months from now.
If something else: tell me what."
```

**"I need to talk to my spouse."**

```
"Respect that. What's your spouse's likely concern — money, time, or something else?

Here's what I'd offer: let me send you a one-page summary your spouse can read, plus a note specifically addressing the common concerns. 

Set up 30 min with them tonight. Then let's reconnect Thursday to decide."
```

### 6. Close

If in:

```
"Great. Next steps:
1. I'll send the Stripe link now for $3k or the first payment of $1,200
2. Once that clears, I'll introduce you to your coach within 48 hours
3. You'll schedule your first 1:1 call within a week
4. Your first article edit will be done within 2 weeks of your first submission
5. Access to our private community goes live today

Any questions before I send the link?"
```

If not ready:

```
"Totally fair. Here's what I want you to do: finish Module 4 by [DATE IN 10-14 DAYS]. Email me when you do. I'll check in, no pressure. If the Accelerator makes sense then, we'll talk. If not, you've got the foundation regardless."
```

### 7. Always book follow-up

```
"Let me put a follow-up on the calendar — [1-2 WEEKS OUT]. I'll send the invite."
```

## Disqualifiers

**Don't sell the Accelerator to:**
- Someone who's committed under 5 hours/week (won't execute)
- Someone still in Module 1 after 30 days (not yet ready)
- Someone in a current life crisis — unless they specifically say "this is my way out"
- Someone who picked a niche they clearly don't care about ("I heard it's profitable")
- Someone who expects results in 3 months (expectations mismatch)
- Someone whose stated budget is $100/month — $3k is outside their means

In those cases: close warm, offer the free resources, set a follow-up.

## Metrics per rep

Track same as AI course:
- Calls scheduled / conducted
- Qualified vs unqualified conversations
- Offers made
- Closes (first-call + follow-up)
- Average close value
- Payment plan vs pay-in-full mix

### Healthy baselines
- 40-60% call show-up rate
- 50-70% qualified rate
- 12-18% close on qualified (lower than AI because affiliate buyers are more risk-averse)
- Another 8-12% close on follow-up
- Cold-to-close overall: 15-25%

## Ethics

Same rules as AI course:
- Never overpromise
- Never pressure a "no"
- Never fabricate scarcity
- Always honor refund policy
- Always disclose realistic outcomes

In affiliate marketing specifically: don't let your sales close rate come from convincing someone to do something that's wrong for them. The 6-month lead time to earnings means bad-fit buyers will request refunds by month 2 if they feel misled — and they're absolutely right to.
