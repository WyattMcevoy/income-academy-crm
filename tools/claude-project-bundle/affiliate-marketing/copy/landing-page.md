## FAQ

**How much will I realistically earn?**
Depends entirely on your effort, niche, and patience. Typical timelines:
- Months 1-3: $0-$50/month
- Months 6-12: $50-$500/month
- Year 1-2: $300-$3,000/month (for dedicated builders)
- Year 2+: $1k-$10k+/month (for serious sites with strong niches)

These are typical. Many earn less. Some earn more. We make no guarantees. See our [earnings disclaimer] for details.

**How much time does this take?**
Plan on 5-10 hours per week minimum for a serious start. Some people do it as a full-time side project (15-20 hrs/week) and move faster.

**Do I need a website already?**
No. The course covers basic setup. A WordPress site with cheap hosting runs under $100/year.

**What niche should I pick?**
We don't tell you. We give you the system to pick one that fits YOUR interests, expertise, and the market. Module 3 is the deep dive on this.

**Is SEO dead?**
No. SEO is harder than it was in 2019. Google's Helpful Content Update (2023) rewarded quality content with demonstrated expertise. We teach post-HCU SEO, not the outdated keyword-stuffing stuff.

**Can AI do all the work?**
No. AI writing tools are useful, but Google de-ranks obvious AI content. We teach human-augmented workflows: use AI as a first-draft assistant, then edit with your own expertise.
