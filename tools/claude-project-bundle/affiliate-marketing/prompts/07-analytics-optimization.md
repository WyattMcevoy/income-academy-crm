# Prompts — Analytics & Optimization

For turning data into action. Most affiliate sites die from neglect, not wrong niche.

## Traffic analysis

### AO-01: Monthly traffic review
```
My site: [NAME + NICHE]
This month's metrics:
- Total sessions: [X]
- Users: [X]
- Page views: [X]
- Avg session duration: [X]
- Bounce rate: [X]
- Top 5 pages by traffic: [LIST]
- Top 5 traffic sources: [LIST]

Compare to last month:
[PASTE]

Analyze:
- What grew? What declined?
- Any surprises?
- Which pages overperformed or underperformed expectations?
- What's the data pointing me to do next?

Honest take. If traffic is flat, don't sugarcoat it.
```

### AO-02: Top-performing content audit
```
These are my top 10 articles by monthly traffic:
[LIST]

For each, estimate:
- How this article currently monetizes (affiliate links to?)
- How it could monetize better (more links? better products? email opt-in?)
- Whether it needs a refresh (age, accuracy)
- Internal link opportunities I'm missing

Prioritize: which ONE article should I spend 2 hours improving this week for maximum impact?
```

### AO-03: Underperforming content review
```
These articles have been live 6+ months with less than 50 monthly visitors:
[LIST]

For each, assess:
- Is the topic competitive?
- Is the article quality high enough?
- Does the title match search intent?
- Is there a keyword mismatch (targeting a keyword that doesn't exist or is too broad)?

Categorize:
- IMPROVE: article has promise, needs refresh
- CONSOLIDATE: merge with better-performing article
- REDIRECT: point its URL to a better article
- DELETE: not worth saving

Be ruthless. Dead weight hurts site quality.
```

## Conversion analysis

### AO-04: Click-through rate by article
```
My top 10 articles and their affiliate CTR (clicks on affiliate links divided by sessions):
[LIST WITH NUMBERS]

Analyze:
- Which are converting above average (>2%)?
- Which are underperforming (<1%)?
- What's different about the winners?

For each underperformer, suggest:
- Is the link placement wrong?
- Are the products not matching the reader intent?
- Is there not enough CTA within the article?
- Is the reader context off (informational content with no buying intent)?
```

### AO-05: Funnel drop-off analysis
```
My typical reader journey:
- Click on article from Google
- Land on article
- Click through to product page (affiliate link)
- Purchase on merchant site

Current conversion rates:
- Article land → affiliate click: [%]
- Affiliate click → purchase: [%]

Given industry benchmarks:
- Article → click: 3-8% is normal
- Click → purchase: 1-5% for major merchants (Amazon), higher (10-30%) for niche products

Which step is my biggest bottleneck? What are 5 specific things I could test?
```

### AO-06: Product mix analysis
```
In the last 30 days, affiliate revenue by product:
[PASTE OR LIST]

Analyze:
- Which 20% of products drive 80% of revenue?
- Are any products significantly underperforming vs traffic?
- Are there products I should promote MORE?
- Are there products I should drop (low conversion, low commission, or poor fit)?

Recommend 3 specific actions: which products to feature more, which to de-emphasize, and which to add.
```

## SEO analysis

### AO-07: Search Console insights
```
My Search Console data (last 90 days):
[PASTE KEY METRICS OR URLS WITH DATA]

Identify opportunities:
- Articles ranking on page 2 (positions 11-20) that could move to page 1 with optimization
- Queries I'm ranking for that I didn't target (evidence of unexpected relevance)
- Queries where I have high impressions but low CTR (title/meta needs work)
- Articles where CTR dropped (competitor took my spot or Google tested new SERP)

Prioritize which 3 articles to optimize this week.
```

### AO-08: Competitor SERP movement
```
Last month my article for "[KEYWORD]" ranked: [POSITION]
This month it ranks: [POSITION]

What might have happened:
- Did Google algorithm update recently? (YES/NO)
- Is a new competitor ranking? (WHO)
- Did an existing competitor improve their content?
- Did my article become outdated?

Recommend action: hold, update, or major rewrite?
```

### AO-09: Rank tracking priorities
```
My top 50 target keywords:
[PASTE LIST WITH CURRENT POSITIONS]

Group them:
- QUICK WINS: positions 4-10 (push to page 1 with small tweaks)
- LONG-TERM BUILDS: positions 11-30 (need substantial improvement)
- LONG-TAIL CAPTURES: pos 1-3 (keep fresh, defend)
- DEAD: positions 30+ (accept or revisit niche strategy)

For each QUICK WIN, suggest the specific optimization likely to help (word count, backlinks, internal links, freshness).
```

## Revenue optimization

### AO-10: Affiliate program performance review
```
I'm in these affiliate programs:
[LIST WITH CURRENT COMMISSION STRUCTURE]

Recent performance:
[PASTE DATA OR ESTIMATES]

Evaluate each:
- ROI (commission × conversion rate × traffic)
- Payment reliability
- Cookie duration
- Brand trust
- Fit with my audience

Should I:
- Prioritize any underutilized programs?
- Negotiate better terms (possible for programs doing $X/month with them)?
- Drop any that aren't pulling weight?
- Look for alternatives to dominant programs (especially if Amazon is 80%+ of revenue — diversify)?
```

### AO-11: "What would I do with 10x traffic" thought experiment
```
My site currently does [X] monthly visitors.
If I had 10× the traffic tomorrow, what would be the bottleneck?

Analyze:
- Could my site handle the traffic technically (hosting, speed)?
- Would affiliate links still convert at current rates, or would I need better conversion paths?
- Would my email list capture handle it?
- Would I be able to produce more content at that scale?

Identify the constraint that would matter most. That's where to invest first.
```

```
Last 10 email sends:
[SUBJECT, OPEN RATE, CLICK RATE, UNSUB RATE — LIST]

Analyze patterns:
- Subject lines that outperformed (what's the pattern?)

```

### AO-13: List health check
```
My email list stats:
- Total subs: [X]
- Active (opened in last 30 days): [Y]
- Active rate: Y/X = [%]

Industry benchmarks:
- Healthy: 30-50% active rate
- Needs attention: 15-30%
- Serious problem: under 15%

Where do I land? What's the action plan?
- Is the content failing to engage?
- Is the list old (subs from 2+ years ago)?
- Is segmentation off (sending everything to everyone)?
- Time for a re-engagement campaign and/or list prune?
```

## A/B testing

### AO-14: A/B test priority list
```
My affiliate business, current performance:
[BRIEF OVERVIEW]

Generate 10 A/B tests I could run, ranked by expected impact:
- Each test: hypothesis, what to change, what to measure, expected lift
- Sample size needed (be realistic)
- Effort to implement (low/medium/high)

Focus on tests where I have enough traffic to reach statistical significance in <30 days.
```

### AO-15: A/B test result analysis
```
Test: [WHAT WAS TESTED]
Variant A (control): [DESCRIPTION + RESULT]
Variant B (treatment): [DESCRIPTION + RESULT]
Sample sizes: [A, B]
Conversion rate: [A %, B %]
Duration: [DAYS]

Analyze:
- Is this result statistically significant? (rule of thumb: need 500+ conversions per variant to be confident)
- What's the likely true lift if rolled out?
- Are there confounding factors (seasonal? traffic source mix?)?
- Should I declare a winner and move on, or test further?

Give me a clear next action.
```

## Data principles

### Decisions > dashboards
If a metric doesn't change what you'd do, don't obsess over it. Focus analysis on metrics that drive action.

### Patterns > single data points
One bad email doesn't mean the strategy is broken. Look at 4-8 weeks of trends before changing direction.

### Statistical significance is real
Don't kill an A/B test variant based on 50 data points. 500+ per variant is a reasonable minimum.

### Small improvements compound
1% better content + 1% better conversion + 1% better placement = 3% blended improvement, which is meaningful over 12 months.

### The niche matters more than the optimization
No amount of optimization will save a bad niche. If the fundamentals are wrong, tweaking is rearranging deck chairs.
