# AI Side Income — Meta Ad Variants

Ready-to-test ad creative for Meta (Facebook + Instagram). All FTC-compliant, all avoiding the keywords that get accounts flagged or suspended.

## Compliance rules baked into all copy

**Never used:**
- "Passive income" / "automated income" / "while you sleep"
- "Guaranteed" / "guaranteed results" / "can't fail"
- "Push button" / "one click" / "autopilot"
- "Make $X/month" / "earn $X in Y days"
- "Beat the system" / "secret method"

**Always used or implied:**
- "Learn" / "skills" / "training" / "education"
- Realistic framing ("part-time," "from home," "at your pace")
- Earnings disclaimer ribbon at bottom of creative

## Targeting guidance (for Meta Ads Manager)

### Audience suggestions (for Advantage+ detailed targeting)

**Interests (stacked)**:
- Side hustles (interest)
- Freelance (interest)
- ChatGPT OR Claude AI OR artificial intelligence (narrowed)
- Retirement (interest — **DON'T** select "retirees" as demographic — that's flagged discrimination)
- Upwork OR Fiverr (interest)
- Self-employment (interest)

**Behaviors**:
- Engaged with business-related content in last 30 days
- Frequent travelers between screens (signals tech comfort)

### Age/gender
Use Meta's "Advantage+ audience" — let the algorithm find people. **Do NOT manually narrow by age or gender for this offer** — even though we have a target demo, Meta's ad system has Special Ad Category rules around employment-related ads that treat "freelance services" with extra scrutiny.

### Placements
- Facebook Feed
- Instagram Feed
- Facebook Stories
- Instagram Stories
- Facebook Right Column (low priority)
- **Skip**: Audience Network (low-quality traffic), Messenger Stories, Reels before you have vertical video ready

### Budget
- $20-50/day per ad set for testing
- Run 3-5 variants simultaneously
- After 3-4 days, kill the bottom 50% and scale the top
- Minimum test spend per variant: $75-100 before making a call

## What to watch for (account safety)

**Green flags (your account is fine)**:
- Low ad disapproval rate (<10%)
- No "Restricted" tags on your ad account
- Consistent delivery at expected volume

**Yellow flags (review your copy)**:
- Ads stuck in "review" for 24+ hours
- Specific ads disapproved with vague reasons
- Relevance score dropping over time

**Red flags (escalate / pause)**:
- Ad account restricted or limited
- Entire Page flagged for policy review
- Business Manager restricted

If you hit red flags: **pause all ads immediately**, review Meta's policy page for "Financial Products and Services" and "Employment Opportunities," and consider contacting Meta Business Help. Don't create a new account — that compounds the problem.

## A/B test structure

### Round 1 — Hook test (Week 1)
Run all 5 variants at equal budgets. Kill bottom 2 after 3 days. Keep top 3.

### Round 2 — Creative format (Week 2)
Take top hook. Test against: static image, video, carousel, text-only.

### Round 3 — Audience (Week 3)
Take top hook + top format. Test against 2-3 audience segments (age-inclusive Advantage+, interest-based, retargeting).

### Round 4 — Scale (Week 4+)
Winner of rounds 1-3 gets 70% of budget. Keep 30% experimenting with new variants.

Total initial test budget: ~$2,000-5,000 before you have a statistical winner.

## Common approval pitfalls

**Pitfall 1**: Using "make money from home" in headline → flagged as employment ad
**Fix**: "Use AI tools to offer freelance services from home" — specific, not promise

**Pitfall 2**: Testimonials with $ amounts → restricted
**Fix**: "Learned skills I didn't have before" (non-specific)

**Pitfall 3**: Before/after lifestyle shots (run-down room → beach) → restricted
**Fix**: Simple working-at-laptop imagery, no aspirational comparisons

**Pitfall 4**: Promising specific timelines ("Land your first client in 14 days!") → restricted
**Fix**: "90-day framework" (framework ≠ guarantee)

**Pitfall 5**: Using "passive income" anywhere → instant rejection
**Fix**: Don't. Ever.
