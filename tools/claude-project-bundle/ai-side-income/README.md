# AI Side Income Starter Kit

**Target audience**: US adults 45–75. Semi-retired or near-retirement. Non-technical. Want extra income from home without hype.

**Compliance posture**: Skill-acquisition framing only. Never promise income. Never use "automation," "passive," or "while you sleep." Always couple any result-example with typical-results disclaimer.

## Offer stack

| Tier | Price | What they get |
|---|---|---|
| **Front-end tripwire** | $47 one-time | 8-module starter course, 200-prompt library, 20 templates, custom Income Academy AI Assistant (GPT/Claude Project) |
| **Continuity** | $19/mo (starts after 7-day trial) | Private community, monthly live Q&A, weekly new-tool/new-prompt drops, quarterly major content refresh |
| **High-ticket back-end** | $2,000-$5,000 | "AI-Augmented Business Launchpad" — 90-day done-with-you program, weekly 1:1 coaching, custom GPT/Claude Project built for their niche |

## Positioning

> "AI is where the internet was in 1996. Your neighbors are already using it. Here's your catch-up plan — no hype, no guarantees, no push-button fantasies. Just the actual skills to land 2-3 paid gigs from home using AI tools."

## Why this works for Income Academy

- **Low production cost**: screen recordings + talking head. No field shoots. ~4-6 hours of total video.
- **AI-generated assets**: prompt library, templates, landing copy all producible by AI tools. Updates cheaply.
- **Naturally recurring**: AI tools change every quarter → content needs refreshes → justifies $19/mo continuity.
- **Different audience slice than Ebay**: people who aren't physical-product types can be AI-service types. Opens a second lane without cannibalizing Ebay funnel.
- **Phone team reuses**: high-ticket upsell uses same sales team with different scripts.

- [ ] Verify full flow with a test `+gh` email

## Risks to manage

1. **FTC "automation" trap** — tightest copy review on every claim. Use `copy/landing-page.md` verbatim or variants reviewed by counsel.
2. **Competitive density rising** — differentiator is demo-specificity. Most AI courses target 25-45. This targets 50-75. Lean into "your neighbors are using it, here's catch-up" framing.
3. **Stripe category sensitivity** — keep copy boring on checkout/Stripe side. Fancy ad copy can live on the landing page; product description on Stripe should be plain: "Educational content about AI tools and freelance services."
