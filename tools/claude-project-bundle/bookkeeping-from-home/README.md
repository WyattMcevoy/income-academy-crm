## Why this course exists

Bookkeeping is the fourth *distinct* income archetype in the bundle (recurring service retainer — not reselling, not content, not gig work). It has:

- **Highest realistic income ceiling** of the 4 courses — 3-5 retainer clients at $400/mo = $1,200-$2,000/mo recurring, makes the $19/mo membership a rounding error
- **Skeptic-friendly legitimacy** — bookkeeping is a 200-year-old profession, not a side hustle. Matters to an audience burned by hype.
- **AI Writing Assistant reuse** — client outreach emails, engagement letters, monthly report narratives — all AI-assisted workflows already in the bundle
- **Clean legal story** — no state licensing required, clear line vs tax prep / accounting / CPA work

## Audience fit (why this wins for 55-75)

- **Schedule alignment**: bookkeeping workload concentrates in "one week mid-month + one week end-of-month" for invoicing/bill-pay. Perfect for retirees wanting predictable, bounded work hours.
- **Experience advantage**: 55-75 buyers often have 30-40 years of workplace bookkeeping exposure already (they've seen QBO/Xero, they've done petty cash, they've reconciled statements). Course fills in the "how to sell this as a service" gap, not the "what is debits and credits."
- **Trust advantage for clients**: small business owners hiring a 60-year-old professional trust them more than a 25-year-old for money management.
