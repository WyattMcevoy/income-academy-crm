### RE: Bookkeeping Services Engagement Letter

Dear [CLIENT NAME],

Thank you for choosing [YOUR BUSINESS NAME] to provide bookkeeping services for [CLIENT BUSINESS NAME]. This letter describes the scope of work, our mutual responsibilities, and the terms of our engagement.

## 1. Scope of Services

I will provide the following services each month:

- **Transaction categorization and coding** for all [#] bank accounts and [#] credit card accounts, based on the chart of accounts we agree on.
- **Bank and credit card reconciliation** to match QuickBooks Online / Xero records to institutional statements.
- **Monthly financial reports**: Profit & Loss statement, Balance Sheet, and Cash Flow statement.
- **Monthly summary narrative**: a plain-English written summary (roughly 100-200 words) of financial performance, trends, and any items you should pay attention to.
- **Accounts Receivable tracking** [if applicable]: invoice aging reports, payment follow-up reminders (not collection activity).
- **Accounts Payable tracking** [if applicable]: bill entry, payment scheduling (but not payment execution).
- **Monthly Q&A availability**: reasonable response to bookkeeping-related questions within 3 business days.

## 2. Services NOT Included

To be explicit, I do **not** provide the following as part of this engagement:

- **Tax preparation or tax advice** of any kind. You should continue to work with your CPA or Enrolled Agent for tax matters.
- **Audit defense** or representation before tax authorities.
- **Payroll processing or tax filing**. If you need payroll, I can recommend providers (Gusto, ADP, Paychex) and coordinate bookkeeping entries from their reports.
- **Financial planning, investment advice, or business consulting** beyond what's implied by accurate bookkeeping reports.
- **Legal services** of any kind.
- **Collection activity** on unpaid invoices (I can track and remind; I cannot legally collect on your behalf).

If you need help with any of the above, I will gladly refer you to qualified professionals.

## 3. Your Responsibilities

To allow me to perform these services accurately and on time, you agree to:

- Provide read-write access to your QuickBooks Online / Xero account.
- Provide read-only access to business bank accounts and credit card accounts (or arrange for automated bank feeds).
- Forward any bills, invoices, receipts, or other financial documents I request within **3 business days** of request.
- Notify me promptly of new bank accounts, credit cards, loans, lines of credit, or other financial accounts.
- Notify me promptly of any significant business events (new contracts, new employees, major purchases, investment activity, legal matters that affect finances).
- Review monthly reports within **7 days** of delivery and flag any concerns.
- Ensure prompt payment of my invoices per Section 6.

## 4. Confidentiality and Data Security

- I will treat all your financial information as confidential.
- I will not share your data with any third party without your written consent, except as required by law.
- I use [PASSWORD MANAGER — e.g., 1Password for Teams] for credential management. I do not store passwords in email.
- I maintain [E&O INSURANCE coverage / other specific security measures].
- Upon termination of this engagement, I will export your QuickBooks / Xero data for you, then revoke my access within 14 days.

## 5. Fees and Payment

- **Monthly retainer**: $[X] per month, covering the scope in Section 1.
- **Payment method**: [ACH debit preferred / credit card accepted / check acceptable]
- **Due date**: 1st of each month, in advance for that month's services.
- **Late fees**: $[Y] if not paid within 10 days of due date; work pauses at 30 days late until caught up.
- **Scope changes**: if you request work outside Section 1 (e.g., clean up prior-year books, special reporting, additional accounts), I will provide a separate proposal at $[Z]/hour or flat fee per project.

## 6. Term and Termination

- This engagement begins [START DATE] and continues month-to-month.
- Either party may terminate with **30 days written notice**.
- Upon termination, you will be invoiced for work completed through the termination date. I will provide an export of your QuickBooks data and a final reconciliation report.
- If terminated due to non-payment, I may retain work product until past-due balances are settled, consistent with state law.

## 7. Limitation of Liability

My liability for any errors or omissions is limited to the fees paid to me in the prior **[3 / 6 / 12] months** of service. I carry Errors & Omissions insurance for additional coverage (see Section 4).

I am not responsible for:
- Errors in data you provide (bank statements, receipts, invoices, etc.)
- Tax penalties or interest resulting from advice given by others (your CPA, IRS, etc.)
- Losses resulting from business decisions based on reports, where those decisions are outside the scope of bookkeeping

## 8. Communication

- **Primary method**: email to [YOUR EMAIL]
- **Response time commitment**: within 3 business days
- **Urgent matters**: [PHONE NUMBER] for time-sensitive items
- **Scheduled reviews**: [MONTHLY / QUARTERLY] check-in call at no additional charge

## 9. Governing Law

This agreement is governed by the laws of the State of [STATE], without regard to conflict of law provisions.

## 10. Entire Agreement

This letter constitutes the entire agreement between us. Any modifications must be in writing and signed by both parties.
