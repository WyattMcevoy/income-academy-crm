# Standard eBay Listing Template

Copy this structure into every eBay listing. Replace [BRACKETS] with your specifics.

---

## Title (80 chars max)

```
[BRAND] [MODEL/PATTERN] Vintage [ITEM TYPE] [SIZE] [ERA] [CONDITION NOTE]
```

Example: `Pyrex Snowflake Blue Vintage Casserole 1.5qt 1960s Lid Included EUC`

---

## Description

```
[OPENING LINE — 1-2 sentences]
A beautiful [ERA] [ITEM] from [MAKER if notable]. [ONE sensory detail
that photos can't show: heft, finish, sound, etc.]

## Condition
[DETAILED CONDITION REPORT — be specific. Mention flaws FIRST, then
strengths. Buyer trust depends on this honesty.]
- [FLAW 1 with exact location and size]
- [FLAW 2 with exact location and size]
- Otherwise: [strengths — clean, all original parts, etc.]

## Dimensions / Measurements
- [MEASUREMENT 1]: [VALUE]
- [MEASUREMENT 2]: [VALUE]
- Weight: [WEIGHT]

## Era / Provenance (if notable)
[YEAR RANGE or decade]. Acquired from [a local estate sale in
YOUR STATE / collector downsizing / etc.]. [ANY STORY that adds
value without fabricating.]

## Shipping
Ships within 1 business day of payment. Carefully packed with
[PACKING DESCRIPTION]. USPS Priority Mail with tracking and
insurance.

## Returns
[YOUR POLICY — e.g., "30-day returns accepted if item differs
significantly from description. Buyer pays return shipping."]

## Questions?
Message me — I respond within a few hours most days. Happy to
provide additional photos or measurements.

---

Please note: this item is vintage / secondhand and may show signs
of age appropriate to its era. See condition report above for any
specific flaws.
```

## Category path

- Home & Garden → Kitchen, Dining & Bar → [SPECIFIC SUBCATEGORY]
- OR: Collectibles → [SUBCATEGORY]
- OR: Antiques → [SUBCATEGORY]

Choose the category that matches how BUYERS search, not where you'd personally file it.

---

## Item specifics (fill every relevant field)

eBay's algorithm favors complete item specifics. Fill in:
- Brand
- Model / Pattern name
- Color
- Material
- Size / Capacity
- Production era
- Country of manufacture
- Condition (matches your description)
- Features / Type

---

## Photos (minimum 6, ideal 10-12)

1. Straight-on front shot (hero image)
2. Side / profile shot
3. Back / bottom shot with maker's marks
4. Close-up of any flaws (don't hide them)
5. Close-up of any signature / stamp / label
6. In-hand or in-use context shot for scale
7-12. Additional angles and details

---

## Pricing strategy

- **"Buy It Now"** (fixed price): use when you know your value. Price 10-15% above your target so there's room for "Best Offer" discounts.
- **Auction**: use when you want price discovery (unusual items, hot categories)
- **Best Offer enabled**: almost always yes. Set auto-decline at 85% of list.
- **Auto-accept at 95%**: saves you the back-and-forth
