# Estate Sale Sourcing — Sales Team Script

**Internal doc for the phone team** when an Estate Sale course member reaches out, or when we're progressing someone from the course toward the eBay Done-With-You graduation tier.

**Never share publicly** — this is internal workflow, not customer-facing.

---

## Purpose

The Estate Sale Sourcing course has a specific upsell path: members who hit consistent sourcing volume graduate to the eBay Reselling Done-With-You program ($2-5k, warehouse + photography + listing + shipping done for them). This script covers how to:

1. Answer questions from current members about the course
2. Identify members who are ready for DWY
3. Have the qualifying call without pressuring unfit applicants
4. Refer appropriately

---

## Call flow

### Opening (Estate Sale member reaching out)

> "Hi [Name], this is [Your Name] from Income Academy. I see you're working through the Estate Sale Sourcing course — how's it going?"

**Listen for**:
- Sourcing volume (low/medium/high)
- Bottlenecks they mention (storage, photography, shipping, time)
- Whether they mention eBay / online sales at all
- Enthusiasm level

### Qualifying questions (organic, not interrogation)

Weave these in:

> "What categories have you been hunting lately?"
> "Roughly how much are you finding per week / month?"
> "Where's most of your time going once you get the items home?"
> "Are you selling anywhere yet — eBay, Facebook, in person?"
> "What's the goal for you — supplement retirement income, or something bigger?"

### Signals: ready for DWY

- Sourcing $1,500+/month consistently
- Spending more time on photography/listing/shipping than sourcing
- Has storage pressure (spouse complaints, overflow into living space)
- Wants to grow but feels the physical limits
- Currently earning $1k-$3k/mo from reselling

### Signals: NOT ready for DWY (keep them in solo mode)

- Just started the course (< 60 days in)
- Sourcing under $500/mo
- Still figuring out which categories fit them
- Says "I just want a side income, not a business"
- Asks lots of "how do I" questions (learning curve not done)
- Cash-strapped (DWY requires inventory investment)

### Advance to DWY pitch (only if ready)

> "Sounds like you're in a great spot. You might be ready for the next level of this — our Done-With-You program. It's where we warehouse your inventory, photograph and list it with professional titles, ship everything from our facility, and handle returns. You focus purely on sourcing. It's not for everyone — by application only — but based on what you're describing, I'd like to walk you through how it works if you're open to it. Fair?"

If they say yes:
- Direct to: incomeacademy.biz/apply-ebay
- Schedule a proper consultation call (not a sales pitch on this check-in call)
- Set expectation: 30-min call, decide fit on both sides, price discussed there

### Decline DWY gracefully (if they're not ready)

> "Honestly, I don't think DWY is the right fit for you right now — your volume would make it expensive relative to the value. Here's what I'd recommend: finish Module 6 of the Estate Sale course (the one on scaling decisions), hit $1,500/mo in sourcing consistently for 3 months, then revisit this conversation. The program will still be here. Keep me posted how it goes."

### Non-DWY support (just helping the member succeed)

If they have a specific question about the course (pricing, negotiation, a specific item), answer directly from the course material. Reference modules by number. Don't pitch anything.

## Common objections + responses

### "I don't want to ship my inventory somewhere I can't see it"

> "Totally fair concern. Our facility is insured, and you get inventory visibility through our member dashboard — you can see every item, where it is, and what it's listed for, in real time. You can also audit on-demand. Think of it like storing valuables at a bank vs. in your garage — different kind of trust, but with accountability built in."

### "What if it doesn't sell?"

> "Inventory that doesn't sell within [timeline] is flagged for discount or return at your choice. You're not locked into a 'we keep it forever' situation. Detailed in the consultation call."

### "How much does DWY cost?"

> "That's a conversation for the consultation call — it depends on your specifics (volume, categories, goals). I can tell you the program is designed to pay for itself — if we can't show you a clear path to ROI on the consultation, we'll tell you directly."

### "Can I do DWY without finishing the course?"

> "In theory yes, but we prefer members finish at least Modules 1-5 first. The course teaches the sourcing side — without that skill, DWY won't produce results because you'd be feeding us low-ROI inventory. The course is the input; DWY is the scale-up."

## Never say

- "Limited spots available" (not a real scarcity lever; feels manipulative)
- "This price is only for today" (no flash-sale pressure)
- "You'll definitely make $X per month" (FTC Biz Opp Rule territory)
- "This is perfect for everyone" (it's not)

---

## Escalation

- Technical question about the course or portal: → support@incomeacademy.biz
- Request for refund (within 7 days): honor immediately, no gauntlet
- Legal/tax question: decline to advise, recommend CPA/attorney
- Wants to speak to the founder: escalate to [YOUR NAME] with a brief summary first
